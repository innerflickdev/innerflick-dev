Our Product

Innerflick is a digital marketing and advertising platform, accessed through a user's web browser and/or mobile device. Innerflick enhances a conversation between businesses and Innerflick user's. The conversation begins as soon as an Innerflick user logs into the Innerflick website or launches the Innerflick app. Innerflick delievers advertisements based on the Innerflick user's personal profile and intrests. After viewing the ad, the Innerflick user completes a quick survey or quiz. The Innerflick user is then rewarded with special incentives from the business based on the content of the ad, and the business can increase online exposure.
